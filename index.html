HumanNet

A distributed human safety network that connects nearby people during emergencies.

Learn the System Open Simulation

The Problem

Delayed Response

Emergency services often take crucial minutes to arrive, during the golden hour when immediate action saves lives.

No Coordination

Nearby people want to help but lack awareness and coordination mechanisms.

Disconnected Communities

Crisis isolates individuals despite crowds around them who could respond instantly.

Limited Participation

Current systems rely only on official responders, underutilizing citizen potential.

The HumanNet Solution

Nearby First Responders

Citizens within 1-2km become instant responders through HumanNet network.

Instant Alerts

SOS broadcasts to volunteers and community simultaneously.

Structured Response

First 3 responders as Guiders coordinate via chat/VoIP, others assist.

Privacy Focused

Minimal data shared, auto-deleted after resolution.

Core Features

🚨 Emergency Alerts

Self-SOS or bystander reports with GPS and minimal info.

👥 Volunteer Network

Guiders (first 3) + Responders coordinate in real-time.

📢 Crowd Reporting

Community reports public dangers like floods or protests.

⚡ Real-Time Awareness

Live feed, location tracking, escalation to public channels.

🛡️ Community Safety

Golden hour focus with rapid community activation.
